Registration for EFSPI regulatory statistics workshop: Frequently asked questions

Published

July 22, 2025

Introduction

Thanks for your interest in the 10th EFSPI regulatory statistics workshop (draft program, status: 11th August 2025)! This site provides FAQs around registration.

Are number of participants limited?

The biozentrum allows for 300 on-site attendees. Registration for face-to-face attendance will be stopped once we reach that number.

Is virtual participation possible?

Yes, the workshop is hybrid.

Venue

The event will take place at the Biozentrum Basel. The exact address is Spitalstrasse 41, Basel and directions are here.

The venue has free WLAN, just connect to Unibas visitor. It will ask for your mobile phone number for confirmation.

Program and registration

Draft program (status: 11th August 2025).

Registration.

Registration fees

280 Euro for face-to-face and 70 Euros for virtual attendance. To register please pay 280 / 70 Euro (depending on whether you attend in-person or virtually) to EFSPI as contribution to the 10th EFSPI regulatory statistics workshop.

IBAN EUR: CH60 0077 0250 6852 9200 3
IBAN CHF: CH87 0077 0250 6852 9200 2
Bank institute: Basler Kantonalbank
Swift: BKBBCHBB
Owner: Basler Biometrische Sektion
Reference: Regulatory 25

Due to this informal registration process we can keep the price low. Therefore we can not accept credit card transfer and we cannot provide you with a formal invoice after the payment. In case you have any question on your payment, please contact Egbert Biesheuvel.

Registration deadline

For face-to-face attendance: 30th August 2025 EOB

For virtual attendance: 9th September 2025 EOB.

Registration platform will be turned off

  • if we have reached maximum capacity of 300 face-to-face participants or
  • for the respective option after the deadline,

whatever happens first.

Confirmation of registration

Some time after registration (it may take 1-2 weeks) you will receive calendar invites for the two and a half days. Please watch out for these invites, this is your confirmation of registration. If you did not receive them there is a high probability you entered your email address with a typo. If that is the case please re-register with the correct email address.

Will the event be recorded?

Yes. Recordings will be posted here, pending speaker approval.

Will slidedecks be shared after the event?

Yes. Slidedecks will be posted here, pending speaker approval.

What block booking options are available for companies?

Face-to-face block booking

We have updated our block bocking offerings compared to previous years:

  • Block booking for face-to-face attendance:
    • 8500 Euro up to 50 participants per company.
    • 10500 Euro for 51 to 70 participants.
    • 12500 Euro for 71 to 90 participants.
    • Beyond 91 face-to-face participants per company we charge 12500 + 120 Euro per each additional participant.

For all face-to-face block bookings unlimited virtual attendees are included as well.

The conference organizers reserve the right to cap the number of registered face-to-face attendees for a company with block booking, in order to keep catering costs under control. This never happened so far though.

Virtual block-booking

  • Block booking for virtual attendance: 2800 Euro, i.e. you reach break-even with this option if at least 40 colleagues from your company register for virtual attendance to the workshop.

Interested in a block booking for your company?

Please reach out to Egbert Biesheuvel if your company is interested in one of these options.

Block booking for face-to-face AND virtual participation: how to register

For 2025 the following companies have registered for a comprehensive block booking so far:

Roche, Boehringer

All colleagues working at Roche or Boehringer do not need to pay a registration fee for face-to-face or virtual participation. You simply register via this form and indicate face-to-face or virtual attendance. You do not need to pay a registration fee, this is covered by your company via the block booking.

To get access to lunches and the wine tasting event, please tick the meals/event you plan to attend. We ask you to be as accurate as possible in indicating your presence - this is for organizational purposes.

Novartis

Block booking for face-to-face and virtual participation:

  • If you wish to attend in person, please contact Bibi Blatna.
  • If you wish to attend virtually, please register for virtual attendance using this form.

Block booking for virtual participation only: how to register

For 2025, Merck KGaA, Bayer, BMS have a a block booking for virtual participation, i.e. all colleagues working at at these companies do not need to pay a registration fee for virtual participation. Face-to-face registration fee remains the same.

You simply register via this form and indicate virtual attendance. You do not need to pay a registration fee, this is covered by your company via a block booking.

My company (Merck KGaA, Bayer, BMS) has a block booking for virtual participation only. I still want to attend face-to-face. How much do I need to pay?

The full registration fee, 280 Euro.
